# Ranger

> You grew up in a secluded area, learning to navigate and learning about the flora and fauna of the area. You are now an expert in surviving in your home area, but even in an unknown area, you can quickly find food and water(if there is any) and can quickly learn to navigate the area and avoid getting lost.

## Names

Rowan, Tamsin, Rook, Tiriel, Bronwyn, Sylara

## Starting Gear

- 3d6 Gold Pieces
- Rations (3 uses)
- Torch (3 uses)
- Knife (d6)
- Bow (d6)
- Padded Leather (1 Armour)
- Tent (fits 2, _bulky_)
